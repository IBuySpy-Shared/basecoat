# Security Findings Triage

Use this reference to classify findings, assign ownership, and track remediation.

## Triage steps

1. Confirm the finding is reproducible and in scope
2. Determine affected asset, environment, and data sensitivity
3. Assign severity based on exploitability and impact
4. Record an owner, remediation plan, and target fix date
5. Retest after remediation and close with evidence

## Suggested remediation targets

| Severity | Target response |
|---|---|
| Critical | Start remediation immediately; mitigate within 24 hours |
| High | Prioritize in the current sprint; remediate within 7 days |
| Medium | Schedule remediation with the owning team; remediate within 30 days |
| Low | Track in backlog and address during planned maintenance |

## Minimum tracking fields

- Finding title and summary
- Severity and business impact
- Affected component, environment, and version
- Reproduction steps or validating evidence
- Owner, due date, and mitigation status
